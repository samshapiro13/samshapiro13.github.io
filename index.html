About Me

My name is Sam Shapiro, and I'm a Computer Science student at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I'm from North Andover, MA and am a graduate of Phillips Academy in Andover, MA. I love playing the drums and sailing, and am a certified lifeguard and camp counselor during the summer. I am in Freshman Council and Grand Challenges at Georgia Tech, both of which require the ability to work in a small-group setting. Through these organizations as well as other programs in high school such as Drumline (pictured here) and working at camp, I've gained valuable leadership and team-building skills to progress the teams I'm involved with further. During these next few years of my life, I would love to have internships and work at a startup. Eventually, I hope to start a company of my own.
